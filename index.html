1.Presentation

Entre Generations is a non-profit association that aims to facilitate and create moments of exchange between the youngest and the elderly. Founded by two teenagers convinced that young people already have an essential role to play in our society, Between Generations sets up activities, correspondence, etc. in order to create a maximum of links between the different generations. This notably involves the use of social networks and the Internet, for example..
